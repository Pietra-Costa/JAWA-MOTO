01 THE PERAK STORY

The Jawa Perak is a striking and formidable motorcycle that has its origins in the 1940s, when the original Jawa brand was founded in Czechoslovakia. Renowned for its legacy of craftsmanship and innovation, Jawa motorcycles swiftly earned a reputation for their outstanding performance, dependability, and unique design.

The Perak model is a significant part of Jawa's heritage, first launched in the late 1940s as a flagship motorcycle celebrated for its exceptional engineering and remarkable performance. The name "Perak," meaning "silver" in Malay, reflects its outstanding quality and prestige. Throughout the years, the Jawa Perak has evolved while maintaining its timeless allure, consistently captivating riders.

Beneath its eye-catching exterior, the Jawa Perak boasts impressive engine performance. It features a potent and refined liquid-cooled 334cc engine that offers an exhilarating riding experience, seamlessly blending power, agility, and smooth handling. In addition to its outstanding performance, the Jawa Perak reflects a high level of craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail. Every element, from the finely-tuned exhaust to the expertly crafted bodywork, exemplifies the brand’s commitment to quality and its dedication to producing motorcycles that are both visually striking and durable.
